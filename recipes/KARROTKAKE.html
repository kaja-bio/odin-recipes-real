Carrot Cake recipe

Ingredients for cake

  • 4 eggs
  • 1.25 cups olive oil
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2cups flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • your heart's fill of cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups grated carrots

Ingredients for frosting

  • 1/2 cup softened butter
  • 225g cream cheese
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 175C.
  2. Beat together eggs, olive oil, white sugar and vanilla extract.
  3. Add flour, baking powder, salt and cinnamon.
  4. Stir in grated carrots.
  5. Bake for 40 minutes.
  6. Prepare frosting by mixing all ingredients.
  7. Frost when cooled. Decorate with fruit.
